## Gotchas recorded

1. **ollama-fork archs don't load upstream:** qwen3.6 / ornith blobs
   (`qwen35`) fail with `rope.dimension_sections` errors. Stick to arch
   `qwen3` (or download dedicated GGUFs) for llama.cpp workers.
2. **GPU contention is real:** the Hermes `local-llm-scout` cron cycles big
   ollama models through this card. The worker unit is therefore
   **start-on-demand** (not enabled) and its launcher refuses to start with
   <18GB VRAM free. **Open policy question for the operator:** who owns the R9700
   until the 7900 XTX lands?
3. qwen3 is a thinking model — small `max_tokens` budgets get eaten by
   reasoning. Use `/no_think` or ≥512 tokens for quick probes.
